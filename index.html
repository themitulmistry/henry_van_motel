About Us

Built on a Foundation of Value and Trust

Henry Van Motel was founded on a simple principle: every traveler deserves a clean, safe, and comfortable place to rest. Since 2008, we have grown from a single roadside property in Ohio to a regional portfolio spanning five states.

We handle acquisition, permitting, renovation, and daily operations in-house, allowing us to maintain standards and move quickly on new opportunities. Our team of 80 hospitality professionals keeps every property running at its best.
